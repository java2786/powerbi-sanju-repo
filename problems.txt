1. Bar Chart
Purpose: Displays the count of movies released in each year.

2. Pie Chart
Purpose: Shows the proportion of movies by category (e.g., Action, Comedy)

3. Line Chart
Purpose: Shows the IMDb rating trend of movies over the years.


4. Stacked Bar Chart
Purpose: Displays the number of movies per category by age group.

5. Area Chart
Purpose: Visualizes the total duration of movies released per year.

6. Histogram
Purpose: Displays the distribution of IMDb ratings.


7. Treemap
Purpose: Displays the count of movies by category and rating

8. Slicer
Purpose: Filters data dynamically.


9. Matrix
Purpose: Displays a tabular view of movies by category and age.
